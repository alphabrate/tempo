Why this project?

The designer of this project said, "Musicians need a powerful tool. Although there are many tools, they are not all user-friendly. Most of them have ads, and others of them have bad UI designs."

How to use?

Go to the site: https://alphabrate.github.io/tempo/. You will see the following UI. (Left/Up)
The blue zone is the top nav and the red zone can be used to clear the tempo. (Right/Down)

Ofcourse, you can listen to your tempo, tap on the blue zone, open the top nav. Press 'Play in : XX BPM'
You will see the following UI. (Left/Up)
If you want to change the sound, tap on the blue zone. (Right/Down)
**You will need to click on the circle to play Your Tempo.**
